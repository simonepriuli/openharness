You can include images in your messages by pasting from the clipboard.

## Pasting an image

1. Copy an image to your clipboard (screenshot, copied image file, etc.)
2. Click in the composer
3. Press `Cmd/Ctrl+V` to paste

The image appears as a preview chip above the text input.

## Removing images

Click the remove button on the image chip, or press **Backspace** when the cursor is after the chip.

## Sending with images

Images are sent alongside your text message. The agent can analyze image content as part of the conversation, depending on the model's vision capabilities.

## Model support

Not all models support image input. If your selected model does not support vision, the image may be ignored or you may see an error. Switch to a vision-capable model in the model switcher.
