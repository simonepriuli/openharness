OpenHarness uses GitHub for sign-in and organization membership for shared features like workflows, secrets, and integrations.

## Sign in with GitHub

1. Launch OpenHarness.
2. Click **Continue with GitHub** on the sign-in screen.
3. Approve the authorization request in your browser.
4. Return to the app — you are signed in.

Your profile appears in **Settings → Account** with your GitHub username and member-since date.

## Organization onboarding

After signing in, you must belong to an organization. On first login you see the onboarding screen with two options:

### Join an organization

If a teammate shared an invite code:

1. Select **Join organization**.
2. Enter the invite code.
3. Click **Join**.

You become a member with access to shared workflows, org secrets, and integrations.

### Create an organization

To start a new team:

1. Select **Create organization**.
2. Enter an organization name.
3. Click **Create**.

You become the organization admin and can invite members, configure secrets, and manage integrations.

## Roles

| Role | Capabilities |
|------|--------------|
| Member | Use org secrets, workflows, integrations; configure personal settings |
| Admin | Manage members, secrets, source control, integrations, and runners |

Admins see additional tabs under **Settings → Organization**, including **Secrets**.

## Sign out

- Click your avatar area in the sidebar footer → **Log out**
- Or use **Settings → Account** → **Log out**

Signing out clears your session. Conversations and projects remain on your machine.
