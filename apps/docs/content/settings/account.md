title: Account settings
description: Your profile and sign out.
---

Open **Settings → Account** to view your signed-in profile.

## Profile information

- GitHub username
- Member since date
- Sign-in method (GitHub)

## Log out

Click **Log out** to end your session. You return to the sign-in screen.

Local projects and conversations remain on your machine after sign out.
